# Key Findings from the Coca-Cola Stock History Analysis (1962-2022). 
## 📊 Perfomance KPIs
**(KO 60-year performance: ~48% win rate, 1.74% volatility across 15K days)**
- In the 15k trading days, the KO has closed ~48% of the time more than it opened. 
- The shares experienced an average daily volatility of 1.74. 
- The analysis covers ~15k trading days spanning 60 years from 1962 to 2022.

## 📈 Volume Analysis
**(140 billion shares traded, consistent monthly liquidity (±5% variation)**
- A total of 140 billion shares was traded in the space of 60 years (1962 to 2022).
- The year 2008 saw the highest average volume of shares sold, ~25 million (4.3% of the 60-year average volume).
- Monthly volumes are almost evenly distributed (±5% variation) across all 12 months.

## 💰 Price Performance
**(15,000% price growth 1962-2022, 2000s strongest decade)**
- The Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) saw the average close price by year rise from 0.04 in 1962 to 60.86 in 2022 (about a 152,050% increase).
- The 2000s experienced a more robust price increase than was obtainable in the 1960s.

## 🌡️ Seasonality Patterns
**(Balanced seasonal trading across quarters/months)**
- The average close price by quarter and by month has shown an almost even spread across the four quarters and the months, indicating balanced seasonal trading.
