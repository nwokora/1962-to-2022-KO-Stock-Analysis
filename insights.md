# 💡 Analysis Insights

## ⏳ Exceptional Long-Term Reliability
KO wins about 48% of days (closes higher than opens) across 15,000 trading days. Daily swings average a tiny 1.74% volatility likely due to demand for beverages which has over time remained steady regardless of economic cycles, proving to be a recession-proof stock with less-risky and predictable cash flows for strategic investors.

## 🚀 Robust Cumulative Growth
15,000% price appreciation (CAGR from 0.04 to 60.86) highlights KO's pricing strength amid inflation, with the 2000s as the standout decade, likely fueled by relentless marketing, leading to a successful global brand expansion and acceptance.

## 💧 Steady Liquidity Flow
Over 60 years, about 140 billion KO shares have changed hands, and trading stays remarkably steady. Monthly volume shifts by only around plus or minus 5%, with no single season or quarter taking over. This smooth, even flow means you can usually buy or sell KO easily any time of year, keeping price gaps and slippage small for both big funds and regular traders.
